STUDENT FINANCE TRACKER
A lightweight, browser-based personal finance app built for students. Track your spending, set a monthly budget cap, and review your transaction history and no account or server needed. All data is saved directly in your browser.

FEATURES

Add transactions : log any expense with a description, amount, category, and date
Dashboard overview : see your total records, total spent, top spending category, and last 7 days at a glance
Search and sort : quickly find any transaction by keyword, sorted by date
Budget cap : set a monthly spending limit and get notified when you approach it
Data management : export your data as JSON, import it back, or clear everything
No login required : everything stays in your browser's local storage

CATEGORIES
Food, Books, Transport, Entertainment, Fees, Other

HOW TO USE 

Open the app in your browser
Go to Add and fill in your transaction details
Check the Dashboard for a summary of your spending
Use the Records section to search or sort past transactions
Go to Settings to set a monthly budget cap or manage your data
DATA AND PRIVACY
All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Use Export JSON in Settings to back up your data regularly.
